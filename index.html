Yosemite Park Guide

Come explore California's beautiful wilderness

Always open. Reservations are not available or required to enter the park.

Not just a great valley, but a shrine to human foresight, the strength of grantie, the power of glaciers, the persistence of life, and the tranquility of High Sierra.


First protected in 1864, Yosemite National Park is best known for its waterfalls, but within its nearly 1,200 square miles, you can find deep valleys, grand meadows, ancient giant sequoias, a vest wilderness area, and much more.
